Activate AMPK
AMPK is an enzyme found in every cell in your body that tells your cells to stop storing fat and start burning it for energy, especially around the abdomen. Over time, declining AMPK levels can cause the accumulation of unhealthy belly fat and even lead to changes in hunger and satiety. Lemme Burn uses a clinically-studied and patented form of Gynostemma extract called Actiponin® to trigger youthful AMPK activity, help fight body fat and promote healthy cellular metabolism.
